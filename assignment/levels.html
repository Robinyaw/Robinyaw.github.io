Levels

In this game, it consists of 8 worlds. Each world have 4 levels. It divided to 7 types. Which are overwold, underground, underwater, castle, bridge, mushroom platforms and dark overworld.

Overworld

The main types of levels are the grassy ones, or overworld. The sky is blue, the main platform are brick blocks and grass and there are trees.

World appeared: 1-1, 2-1, 4-1, 5-1, 7-1, 8-1, 8-3

Underground

Underground areas are dark, and feature many secrets such as getting above the ceiling, and the warp zones. Every sprite in here has a blue or other darker color, including Mario. There are tons of blocks, which means it's a good idea to be Super Mario in here.

World appeared: 1-2, 4-2, 5-2

Mushroom plattforms

These types of levels take place on many different types of huge mushrooms.

World appeared: 1-3, 3-3, 4-3, 5-3, 6-3

Castle

Castles, or lava areas, will be the last type of level in each world. In these areas, there will be many lava pits, and castle bricks surrounding you.

World appeared: All worlds level 4

Bridge

The bridge levels feature a huge outside bridge, with occasional grassy platforms.

World appeared: 2-3, 7-3

Dark Overworld

Sometimes, the grassy/overworld will be nighttime. This does not effect the gameplay in any way.

World appeared: 3-1, 3-2, 6-1, 6-2

Underwater

Underwater areas are very blue, and have a lot of coral and water plants. This is the only area where the gameplay increasingly changes. Instead of running and jumping, Mario will now swim.

World appeared: 2-2, 7-2
